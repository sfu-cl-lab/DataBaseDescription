NBA

A database with information about basketball matches from the National Basketball Association. Lists Players, Teams, and matches with action counts for each player.

  • Aggregating Predictions vs. Aggregating Features for Relational Classification. Oliver Schulte and Kurt Routley (2014). IEEE Symposium Series on Computational Intelligence, CI and Data Management Track (IEEE SSCI 2014). BibTeX

PremierLeague

A database with information about football matches from the UK Premier League, the x season. Lists Players, Teams, and matches with action counts for each player.
